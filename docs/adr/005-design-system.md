# ADR-005: Quiet editorial utility

**Status:** accepted · **Date:** 2026-07-20

## Decision

Use a warm paper surface, sumi-tinted ink, restrained vermilion, Shippori Mincho display type, Zen Kaku Gothic New body type, IBM Plex Mono for code, hairline rules, deliberate negative space, and low-motion interaction.

The visual influence is not marketed as a cultural identity. Decorative motifs, fabricated translations, gradients, glass, faux paper texture, and generic generated-site structures are excluded.

## Consequences

All color and typography values are semantic tokens. Contrast is unit-tested, motion respects reduced-motion preferences, and the app is verified from 320px through desktop.
